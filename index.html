About Us

Nurturing Young Minds With Love & Care

At Ducklings Preschool, we believe every child is unique and special. Located in the heart of Kalkere, Krishnarajapuram, we've been nurturing young minds for over a decade, creating a safe and stimulating environment where curiosity thrives.

Our dedicated team of experienced educators follows a child-centered approach, blending structured learning with creative play to develop confident, curious, and caring individuals ready for their educational journey ahead.
